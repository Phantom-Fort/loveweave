How to Play

1. Two ways to start
• Sign in with Google to invite partners by email (they get an in-app notification).
• Or Continue without login and use a Game ID (classic mode).
2. Play together in real-time
  • Our Story — Write your shared story. Choose a tone (romantic, fun, future, spicy).
  • Daily Spark — Answer the daily prompt. See your partner's answer appear live.
  • Love Notes — Send quick loving messages that both see instantly.
  • Guess About Me — Write something about your partner. They mark True/False and you earn points.
  • Bridge the Gap — 500 categorized questions (Emotional, Conflict, Values, Intimacy, etc.). Filter by category.
Everything stays in sync automatically. Have fun weaving your love!
